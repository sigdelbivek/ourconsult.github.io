How it works

Location independent - All that is needed is network connection.


1

Select your Patient

Provider logs in and sends email and/or text to the patient with a link to sign in.

2

Choose Patients then talk to provider

Patient clicks on the link from any browser and is placed in the virtual lobby.

3

Communicates with Patient

Provider brings the patient in and consultation begins.

4

Online chat with provider to patient

After-visit summary is automatically generated and shared with the patient.
